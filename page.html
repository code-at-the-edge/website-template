What Do You know About Beans?

Beans are legumes -- a special kind of plant that does an important job in nature. Legumes are nitrogen-fixers. They take the element nitrogen from out of the air and make it into proteins and vitamins that we can eat. WHen the beans die, they leave nitrogen behind in the soil for other plants to use.

When we plant beans, lentils, or peas, we make a healthy and delicious snack for ourselves, but we also make our fields healthier and stronger.

They are very nutritious. They will make you so healthy. Hoouray for beans!
